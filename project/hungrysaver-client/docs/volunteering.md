# Volunteering on Hungry Saver

## Who Can Become a Volunteer

Anyone willing to help their community can register as a volunteer on Hungry Saver. There are no specific age, education, or background requirements. The platform welcomes individuals who are committed to making a positive impact in their local community.

## Registration and Approval Process

The volunteer registration process involves several steps:

1. Account Creation: Prospective volunteers create an account on the platform using their email address.

2. Profile Completion: Volunteers provide personal information including:
   - Full name
   - Contact details
   - Location or city where they want to volunteer
   - Availability and preferences

3. Application Submission: Volunteers submit their application for review.

4. Admin Review: Platform administrators review each volunteer application to verify information and ensure the volunteer meets basic requirements.

5. Approval: Once approved, volunteers receive access to their location-specific dashboard where they can view and accept tasks.

6. Pending Status: During the review period, volunteers see a pending approval page with information about the initiatives they will support.

The approval process typically takes 24 to 48 hours. Volunteers are notified via email once their application is approved or if additional information is required.

## Volunteer Responsibilities

Volunteers have several key responsibilities:

- Accepting tasks that match their availability and capacity
- Communicating clearly with donors and beneficiaries
- Picking up donations from donor addresses in a timely manner
- Handling donations with care and respect
- Delivering donations safely to beneficiaries or designated locations
- Providing feedback after completing deliveries
- Maintaining confidentiality of donor and beneficiary information
- Following safety guidelines for handling different types of donations

## How Pickup and Delivery Works

The pickup and delivery process follows these steps:

1. Task Acceptance: Volunteers browse available tasks in their assigned city and accept tasks they can complete.

2. Contact Information: Once a task is accepted, volunteers receive contact details for the donor, including phone number and pickup address.

3. Pickup Coordination: Volunteers contact donors to arrange a convenient pickup time and confirm the pickup address.

4. Pickup Execution: Volunteers arrive at the donor's location, verify the donation items, and collect them.

5. Status Update: Volunteers mark the task as picked up in the system.

6. Delivery: Volunteers transport the donation to the beneficiary or designated delivery location.

7. Delivery Confirmation: Upon delivery, volunteers mark the task as delivered and can submit feedback.

8. Completion: Volunteers provide feedback about the delivery experience, including any photos or notes, and mark the task as completed.

## Address Sharing and Privacy

Address sharing on Hungry Saver is handled with care:

- Donor addresses are only shared with volunteers who have accepted a specific task
- Beneficiary addresses are shared only when necessary for delivery
- Contact information is shared securely through the platform
- Volunteers are expected to use contact information only for task-related communication
- All personal information must be kept confidential and not shared outside the platform

The platform prioritizes privacy while ensuring volunteers have the information they need to complete tasks successfully.

## Code of Conduct

Volunteers are expected to follow a code of conduct that includes:

- Treating all donors and beneficiaries with respect and dignity
- Maintaining punctuality for scheduled pickups and deliveries
- Communicating clearly and professionally
- Handling donations with care and ensuring they reach beneficiaries in good condition
- Reporting any issues or concerns to platform administrators
- Not accepting gifts or compensation beyond the satisfaction of helping others
- Respecting the privacy and confidentiality of all parties involved
- Following safety guidelines and local regulations

Violations of the code of conduct may result in suspension or removal from the platform.
