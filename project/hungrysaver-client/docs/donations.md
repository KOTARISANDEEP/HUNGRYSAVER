# Donations on Hungry Saver

## Who Can Donate

Anyone can become a donor on Hungry Saver. Donors can be individuals, families, businesses, or organizations who have surplus resources they wish to share with the community. There are no restrictions on who can donate, as long as they can provide items or resources that meet the platform's safety and quality standards.

## Types of Donations

Hungry Saver accepts various types of donations across its six initiatives:

- Food donations through Annamitra Seva
- Clothing, furniture, and electronics through PunarAsha
- Educational materials and financial support through Vidya Jyothi
- Essential supplies and support through Suraksha Setu
- Emergency resources through Raksha Jyothi
- Shelter support through Jyothi Nilayam

Donors can choose which initiative they want to support based on their available resources and interests.

## Donation Process

The donation process follows these steps:

1. Registration: Donors must create an account on the platform and complete their profile with basic information.

2. Initiative Selection: Donors select the initiative they want to support from the available options.

3. Form Completion: Donors fill out a donation form specific to their chosen initiative. The form includes:
   - Details about the items being donated
   - Location and pickup address
   - Contact information
   - Any special instructions for volunteers

4. Submission: Once the form is submitted, the donation is created and becomes visible to volunteers in the donor's city.

5. Volunteer Acceptance: A volunteer in the donor's city accepts the donation task.

6. Pickup: The volunteer picks up the donation from the donor's specified address.

7. Delivery: The volunteer delivers the donation to the beneficiary or designated location.

8. Completion: Once delivery is confirmed, the donation is marked as completed, and the donor receives feedback from the volunteer.

## Food Donation Safety

Food donations must meet specific safety requirements:

- Prepared food must be fresh and safe for consumption
- Food should be properly packaged to maintain hygiene
- Perishable items should be handled according to food safety guidelines
- Donors should ensure food items are within their safe consumption period
- Volunteers are trained to handle food donations safely during transport

The platform prioritizes food safety to protect both volunteers and beneficiaries.

## What Happens After Donation

After a donor submits a donation:

1. The donation appears in the volunteer dashboard for the donor's city
2. Volunteers can view donation details and accept tasks that match their availability
3. Once accepted, the volunteer receives the donor's contact information and pickup address
4. The volunteer coordinates with the donor to arrange pickup
5. After pickup, the volunteer delivers the donation to the beneficiary
6. Upon delivery, the volunteer submits feedback and proof of delivery
7. The donor can track the status of their donation through their dashboard

## Tracking Donation Status

Donors can track their donations through the Donor Dashboard. The dashboard shows:

- Pending donations awaiting volunteer acceptance
- Accepted donations that have been picked up by volunteers
- In-transit donations being delivered
- Completed donations with volunteer feedback

Each donation status update is visible in real-time, allowing donors to see the progress of their contributions from submission to completion.
