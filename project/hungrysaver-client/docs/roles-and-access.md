# Roles and Access on Hungry Saver

Hungry Saver has four distinct user roles, each with specific capabilities and limitations. Understanding these roles helps users know what they can and cannot do on the platform.

## Donor

### What Donors Can Do

- Create and submit donation requests for any of the six initiatives
- Choose which initiative to support based on available resources
- Specify pickup location and contact information
- Track the status of their donations in real-time
- View volunteer feedback after donation completion
- See their donation history and impact
- Claim community requests to fulfill specific needs
- Access the chatbot for support and information

### What Donors Cannot Do

- Cannot directly deliver donations to beneficiaries
- Cannot access volunteer dashboards or tasks
- Cannot approve or reject volunteer assignments
- Cannot modify donation details after a volunteer has accepted the task
- Cannot access community support request submission features
- Cannot view other donors' information or donations

## Volunteer

### What Volunteers Can Do

- View available donation tasks in their assigned city
- Accept or reject donation tasks based on availability
- View and accept community support requests
- Access donor contact information and pickup addresses for accepted tasks
- Update task status from accepted to picked up to delivered
- Submit feedback and proof of delivery after completing tasks
- Track their task history and completion rate
- Access the chatbot for support and information

### What Volunteers Cannot Do

- Cannot access tasks outside their assigned city
- Cannot modify donation details or donor information
- Cannot approve or reject other volunteers
- Cannot create donations on behalf of donors
- Cannot access admin functions
- Cannot view other volunteers' personal information
- Cannot submit community requests as beneficiaries

## Community Support

### What Community Support Users Can Do

- Submit requests for assistance across all six initiatives
- Specify urgency levels for their requests
- Track the status of their submitted requests
- View their request history
- Update request details when necessary
- Access the chatbot for support and information

### What Community Support Users Cannot Do

- Cannot access donor or volunteer dashboards
- Cannot see donor information or volunteer details
- Cannot directly contact donors or volunteers outside the platform
- Cannot approve or reject volunteer assignments
- Cannot create donations
- Cannot access admin functions
- Cannot view other community members' requests

## Admin

### What Admins Can Do

- Review and approve volunteer applications
- Review and approve community support requests
- Monitor platform activity and user interactions
- Access all user information for platform management
- Manage user accounts and permissions
- View system-wide statistics and reports
- Resolve disputes or issues between users
- Update platform settings and configurations

### What Admins Cannot Do

- Cannot create donations on behalf of users
- Cannot directly deliver donations or complete volunteer tasks
- Cannot modify donation details after submission
- Cannot access the chatbot as a regular user
- Cannot bypass platform security or privacy measures

## Role-Based Access Summary

Each role has access only to features and information relevant to their function on the platform. This design ensures:

- Privacy protection for all users
- Clear boundaries between different user types
- Efficient workflow for each role
- Security of personal information
- Appropriate access to necessary features

Users can only access features and information that align with their role, maintaining the integrity and security of the platform.
