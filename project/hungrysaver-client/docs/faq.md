# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Hungry Saver only for food?

No. Hungry Saver supports multiple types of community needs through six different initiatives. While food donation through Annamitra Seva is one initiative, the platform also supports education through Vidya Jyothi, emergency response through Raksha Jyothi, material donations through PunarAsha, community support through Suraksha Setu, and shelter support through Jyothi Nilayam.

## Is my donation safe?

Yes. Donations are handled through verified volunteers and trusted partners. All volunteers go through an approval process before they can accept tasks. The platform maintains transparency in the donation process, allowing donors to track their contributions from submission to delivery. Volunteers are expected to follow safety guidelines and handle donations with care.

## How are beneficiaries chosen?

Beneficiaries are identified through NGOs, community requests, and verified needs. The platform connects donors with beneficiaries based on location, need, and availability of resources. Community support requests are reviewed and approved before becoming visible to volunteers and donors. The matching process ensures that resources reach those who need them most.

## Can NGOs join?

Yes. NGOs can join Hungry Saver to request support for their programs and beneficiaries. NGOs can submit requests for various types of donations and coordinate with volunteers for delivery. The platform helps NGOs access resources they need to serve their communities effectively.

## How do volunteers get addresses?

After accepting a task, volunteers receive the pickup address and contact information through the platform. Addresses are shared securely only with volunteers who have accepted specific tasks. Volunteers use this information to coordinate pickup and delivery, and they are expected to maintain confidentiality of all address information.

## Is Hungry Saver free to use?

Yes. Hungry Saver is free for all users. Donors, volunteers, and community members can use the platform without any fees. The platform operates as a community service, connecting those who can help with those who need assistance.

## How long does it take for a donation to be picked up?

The time it takes for a donation to be picked up depends on volunteer availability in the donor's city. Once a volunteer accepts a donation task, they coordinate with the donor to arrange a convenient pickup time. Response times vary by location and volunteer availability.

## What happens if no volunteer accepts my donation?

If no volunteer accepts a donation within a reasonable time, the donation remains visible in the volunteer dashboard. Donors can also choose to claim community requests directly, connecting their donations with specific needs. In some cases, platform administrators may help coordinate volunteer assignments.

## Can I donate anonymously?

The platform requires basic contact information for coordination purposes, but donors can choose how much information to share with volunteers. Donors' personal details are kept confidential and shared only with volunteers who accept their specific donation tasks.

## How do I track my impact?

Donors can track their impact through the Donor Dashboard, which shows donation history, status updates, and volunteer feedback. The dashboard provides real-time updates on donation progress from submission to completion, allowing donors to see the direct impact of their contributions.

## What if I need to cancel a donation?

If a donation has not been accepted by a volunteer, donors can typically cancel or modify their donation. Once a volunteer has accepted a donation, coordination should be done directly with the volunteer. Platform administrators can assist with cancellation requests if needed.

## Are volunteers trained?

Volunteers receive guidelines and safety information through the platform. While formal training may vary, all volunteers are expected to follow safety guidelines, handle donations with care, and maintain professional communication. The platform provides resources and support to help volunteers fulfill their responsibilities effectively.

## How do I report an issue?

Users can report issues through the platform's support channels or by contacting platform administrators. The platform has mechanisms in place to address concerns, resolve disputes, and ensure a positive experience for all users.
